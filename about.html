About Insurance Allied USA

We exist to make insurance simple, transparent, and accessible for every American.

Our Mission

Insurance Allied USA was founded with a single purpose: to help ordinary Americans understand the insurance products that protect their families, homes, businesses, and futures.

Insurance is one of the most important financial tools in anyone's life — yet it's also one of the most confusing and least transparent industries in America. We're changing that.

We publish clear, unbiased, expertly-researched guides on health insurance, auto insurance, life insurance, homeowners insurance, Medicare, and more — all completely free to read.

We never accept payment from insurance companies to promote their products. Our readers' trust is everything to us.
